Unit 8: FIRST Robotics Competition

9-Week Engineering Design Project

About This Unit

Students participating in the FIRST Robotics Competition will use their team experience as coursework for this marking period. You'll apply the full engineering design process while building a competition robot, documenting your individual contributions through a comprehensive portfolio system.
