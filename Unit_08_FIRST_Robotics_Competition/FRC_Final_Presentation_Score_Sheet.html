Criterion Performance Descriptors Score Notes
1 — Technical Content / 10
How It Works
6 Clearly explains how their subsystem works mechanically, electrically, or programmatically; accurate and specific.
4–5 Correct explanation but missing key detail or depth.
2–3 General or partially inaccurate; hard to tell how the subsystem actually functions.
0–1 Vague, incorrect, or no technical explanation.
/6
Design Decisions
4 Explains why key choices were made (materials, code approach, mechanism) and how they connect to robot performance.
2–3 Mentions a decision but no rationale or connection to outcomes.
0–1 No design decisions discussed.
/4
2 — Personal Contribution / 5
Their Role
5 Clearly identifies what they personally built, coded, or designed; distinguishes their work from the team's.
3–4 Describes contribution but stays general ("I helped with…").
1–2 Unclear what they specifically did versus the broader team.
0 No personal contribution identified.
/5
3 — Challenges & Problem-Solving / 5
Challenge & Response
5 Describes a specific real problem encountered, explains the reasoning behind their solution, and reflects on the outcome or what they'd do differently.
3–4 Mentions a challenge; solution or reflection is thin.
1–2 Challenge noted only in passing; no explanation of how it was addressed.
0 No challenges discussed.
/5
4 — Communication & Delivery / 3
Clarity & Confidence
3 Clear and confident; maintains eye contact; easy to follow without technical background.
2 Mostly clear; some reliance on notes or filler words but recovers.
1 Difficult to follow; reads from notes or speaks too quietly to hear.
0 Unable to present.
/3
5 — Organization & Time / 2
Structure & Timing
2 Fits within 3–5 minutes; presentation has a clear beginning, middle, and end.
1 Over or under time by more than 1 minute, or hard to follow the structure.
0 Significantly off time or no discernible structure.
