Weekly Reflection (Due Fridays)

  • 3+ tasks with dates ("I designed...", "I built...")
  • 2+ photos of MY work
  • Challenges I faced
  • How I solved them
  • 3 specific goals for next week

= 20 points each week

Writing About YOUR Work

Good Examples:

  • "I designed the intake roller spacing in CAD"
  • "I wired the limit switches on the arm"
  • "I tested 5 different wheel materials"
  • "I wrote the autonomous pickup routine"

Avoid These:

  • "Helped the team"
  • "Worked on the robot"
  • "We built stuff"
  • "Did some wiring"

Photo Evidence Ideas

  • You at your workstation
  • Parts YOU fabricated/assembled
  • Wiring YOU completed
  • YOUR CAD screenshots
  • Test setups you created
  • Before/after comparisons

Tip: Take photos WHILE working. Check timestamps!
