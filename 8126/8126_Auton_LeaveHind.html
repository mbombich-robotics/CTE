Two-Piece Auto — Sequence

  • 1
    Pre-spin & aim Shooter spins to 3,500 RPM on autonomousInit(). PID controller rotates robot back toward speaker. Feeds when aligned (<2°) and at RPM.
  • 2
    First shot Indexer and floor run for 3 s; hopper oscillates (0.02 → 0.19) to agitate game pieces.
  • 3
    Drive to pickup (0.61, 0.68 m) Proportional speed ramp (max 50%); shooter holds cap RPM during transit. Hopper deploys, collector runs.
  • 4
    Collect — 3 s dwell Stationary at pickup; collector running. Robot holds 180° heading.
  • 5
    Drive to shoot (2.46, 2.69 m) Shooter re-enables full RPM en route. Rotation PID re-locks heading.
  • 6
    Second shot Re-aims; feeds when conditions met. 3 s shooting window; auto ends.
