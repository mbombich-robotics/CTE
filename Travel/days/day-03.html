Tips & Notes

  • 🌅 Sunrise at Mont Saint-Michel is at 6:26 AM — it's only 7 minutes from the VRBO, so early risers can do a quick out-and-back before the group breakfast without eating into the day's schedule. Totally optional; everyone else just meets at the regular 9:00 AM departure.
  • If you do the sunrise run: bring a flashlight/headlamp (it's dark when you leave) and dress warm — Normandy mornings are cool even in July. Parking gates likely aren't staffed that early, so it may end up free.
  • 🅿️ Crit'Air sticker required to drive into central Paris on a weekday — check the van's windshield before you go; order online in advance if it's missing (critair.gouv.fr, ~€3.70, allow a few days for it to arrive by mail).
  • This is now a real marathon day: ~5.5 hours of driving plus 4 hours in Paris. Swap drivers, and don't skip the coffee stops.
  • At Mont Saint-Michel you don't need a ticket for photos — the classic causeway and ramparts shots are all free and outside the abbey.
  • Buy Big Bus Paris tickets online in advance — usually cheaper than at the stop, and you skip the ticket-counter line.
  • Paris traffic is heavy midday — budget extra time getting in and out, especially on the A13/A86 approach.
  • Dinner comes before hotel check-in tonight — the Best Western is your last stop, so there's no need to unpack and head back out.

Photo Suggestions

  • Sunrise (early risers): Mont Saint-Michel silhouetted against the pre-dawn sky, mist over the bay — likely the best light of the whole trip.
  • Mont Saint-Michel (mid-morning): the classic causeway/low-tide silhouette shot; the abbey spire from outside the ramparts.
  • Eiffel Tower: from the Champ de Mars or Trocadéro — the classic Paris shot.
  • From the open-top bus: Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, Notre-Dame as you pass.
