Tips & Notes

  • ARRIVE EARLY at Mont Saint-Michel — crowds build through the afternoon.
  • The Abbey has 350+ steps and is NOT wheelchair accessible.
  • This is the longest drive of the trip — plan coffee breaks and swap drivers.
  • Reims check-in is flexible; the hotel is open late, so don't stress the 7 PM arrival.
  • Save some energy tonight — tomorrow eases into the wine region.

Photo Suggestions

  • Mont Saint-Michel: the classic causeway/low-tide silhouette shot; the abbey spire from the ramparts; the view from the Grand Degré steps.
  • Reims (evening): Notre-Dame de Reims Cathedral façade lit up at dusk if you pass by before dinner.
