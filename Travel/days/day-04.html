Tips & Notes

  • This is a deliberately lighter day — a rest day built in after Day 3's marathon. Nobody has to do anything they don't want to.
  • Wine tasting and the vineyard walk are fully optional — two of you aren't into it, and that's completely fine. Split up: some explore the village, others just relax at the VRBO.
  • Use part of the afternoon to print/confirm your Neuschwanstein tickets for Day 6 — that visit has strict timing (see Day 6's tips) — and talk through Day 5's Aschaffenburg plan as a group.
  • Early check-in (11 AM) means a genuinely long afternoon — good for actual downtime, laundry, or just sitting still for a few hours.
  • Westhofen is small and walkable if anyone wants to wander — leave the car once you're in.
  • Decide on champagne tour vs. cathedral (or neither) before you leave the hotel — the tour needs a booked time slot.

Photo Suggestions

  • Reims: Notre-Dame de Reims Cathedral west façade and rose window; the champagne cellars if you tour Taittinger.
  • Westhofen (optional): vineyard rows at golden hour, the village square and half-timbered wine-house architecture — for whoever wanders out.
