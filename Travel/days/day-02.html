Tips & Notes

  • 🚗 This is the longest driving day of the trip so far — ~5 hours round-trip, since the beaches are ~1.5 hrs from the VRBO, not a quick local hop. Swap drivers if you can.
  • Pack a picnic lunch from a grocery store before heading out.
  • Bring sunscreen and water bottles — you'll be outdoors most of the day.
  • The 7:00 AM breakfast and 7:30 AM departure aren't optional — they're needed to fit all four stops with real time at each.
  • These are emotional, historical sites — build in time for reflection, not just photos.
  • If the day feels too long, consider dropping the Utah Beach Museum — it's the most out-of-the-way stop and skipping it saves roughly 1.5 hours combined (drive + visit).

Photo Suggestions

  • Normandy American Cemetery: the rows of white crosses and Stars of David overlooking the bluff; the reflecting pool and memorial colonnade.
  • Omaha Beach: a wide low-tide shot toward the bluffs; the "Les Braves" sculpture.
  • Pointe du Hoc: the bomb craters and cliff-edge German bunkers.
  • Utah Beach: the Higgins boat monument and museum exterior.
