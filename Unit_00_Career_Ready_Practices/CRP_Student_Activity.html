Day 3 — Your Engineering Career Plan

Standards: PM1 + PM2  |  Submit in the Portfolio as Deliverable 0

Use your research from Days 1 and 2 to answer the five prompts below. Write 2–4 sentences per prompt. When you are done, open your portfolio and submit your responses as Deliverable 0 — Career Ready Practices Reflection.
Prompt 1 — Career Interest

Which career or trade interests you most, and why? Connect your answer to something specific you built or learned in this class.

Prompt 2 — Education Path

What education or training would you need to pursue this career? Be specific to your career (apprenticeship, associate degree, bachelor's, certification, etc.) and describe your plan for getting there.

Prompt 3 — Financial Reality

Based on your research, what would your monthly take-home be on a starting salary in your field? Was that more or less than you expected, and why does that gap matter?

Prompt 4 — Financial Goal

Name one specific financial goal you would set in your first year of working. Be specific — include an amount and a purpose (not just "save money").

Prompt 5 — Career Ready Skills

Name one Career Ready skill you practiced in this class this year and give a specific example of when you used it.

When your prompts are complete, open your portfolio and submit them as Deliverable 0 — Career Ready Practices Reflection.
