The Challenge

Build the Tallest Free-Standing Tower!

Using 20 sticks of spaghetti and 1 bag of marshmallows

Rules

  • Must be free-standing (no support)
  • Must stand for 10 seconds
  • Can break spaghetti into pieces
  • All materials stay with the tower
  • Time limit: 18 minutes

Scoring

Height measured from base to highest point

Tallest tower wins! 🏆

Important: Tower must stand unsupported when time is called!

Structural Engineering Basics

What makes towers strong and stable?

📐

Triangles

Most stable shape in engineering

⚖️

Wide Base

Lower center of gravity = more stable

🎯

Weight Distribution

Balance weight throughout structure

Key Concepts

  • Tension: Forces pulling apart (spaghetti is weak in tension)
  • Compression: Forces pushing together (spaghetti is strong in compression)
  • Load bearing: Distributing weight to prevent collapse

Planning Phase (5 minutes)

1 Ask: What's the challenge?

Build tallest free-standing tower that lasts 10 seconds

2 Research: What do we know?

  • Triangles are strongest shapes
  • Wide base increases stability
  • Spaghetti is rigid but fragile
  • Marshmallows are flexible connectors

3 Imagine: Brainstorm designs

As a team, sketch 2-3 possible tower designs

Consider: pyramid, tripod, square base, lattice structure

4 Plan: Choose Your Design

Team Decision Time (3 minutes)

Discuss and select your best design

Evaluation Questions

  • Will it be stable?
  • Can we build it in 18 minutes?
  • Does it use our materials well?
  • How tall can we make it?

Build Strategy

  • Start with base
  • Build upward in sections
  • Test stability as you go
  • Save time for adjustments

Activity: Draw your final design and assign building tasks

5 Create: Build Your Tower!

🏗️ BUILD TIME: 18 MINUTES

Ready... Set... BUILD!

Building Tips

  • Push spaghetti fully into marshmallows
  • Use fresh marshmallows (not squished)
  • Build on flat surface
  • Test frequently
  • Work carefully but quickly

Common Mistakes

  • Building too fast (collapse!)
  • Top-heavy design
  • Weak connections
  • Using all materials at start
  • Not testing until the end

Remember: Communication and teamwork are key!

6 Test: Does It Stand?

Final Test Requirements

  • Remove all hands from tower
  • Tower must stand for 10 seconds
  • No leaning on walls or tables
  • Measure from base to highest point
📏

Height Measurement

Measured in centimeters or inches

⏱️

Stability Test

Must stand unsupported for 10 seconds

Record Your Results

  • Final tower height
  • Did it stand for 10 seconds? (Yes/No)
  • Team ranking
